Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

  - It was created for the reason to help people with ADHD, people that are predominantly inattentive or hyperactive and/or impulsive to learn. And instead of immediately discouraging them that learning is hard, Focus Bear helps them with their disadvantages by simply being a partner that slowly but surely assists them to learn.

What problem is Focus Bear solving?

  - The problem with people that have some form of ADHD be assisted through the Focus Bear App. Additionally, Focus Bear Developers are also adapting the app to be ever more friendly to the users.

Why do you think this mission is important?
  
  - Because of social media sites, our attention span have gotten lower, ever more so to the people with Disadvantages like ADHD, which social media sites can effectively prey on people with said condition. Thus making them dependent on the dopamine of an escape from their trouble learning through sheer procrastination, which lead to no confidence in learning.
